About Me

Hi, I'm Chitaha, a data consultant with a passion for using data to make a positive difference. I know that making sense of data can be tough, especially for organizations led by women, LGBTQ+ communities, Indigenous groups, and other marginalized voices. Each of these organizations has a unique story and mission, and sometimes the data can feel like a puzzle that just doesn't fit.

The Problem: It can be overwhelming to navigate the world of data. Without the right tools and insights, important information can get lost, making it harder to achieve your goals and create the impact you want. Many organizations don't realize how much potential their data holds or how it can be used to drive their mission forward.

The Solution: That's where I come in. I’m here to help you unlock the potential of your data. By tailoring data tools and insights to fit your specific needs, I can help you turn that puzzle into a clear picture. Whether you're looking to understand your audience better, measure the impact of your programs, or make data-driven decisions, I can provide the support you need.

Why Work With Me? I believe in the power of data to tell stories and drive change. My approach is not just about numbers; it's about understanding your unique context and goals. I work closely with you to ensure that the data solutions we develop are meaningful and actionable. Together, we can make sure your data works for you, helping you to achieve your mission and make a lasting impact.

Who Can Benefit? If you're part of an organization that feels overwhelmed by data or unsure how to use it effectively, my services are for you. Whether you're a small nonprofit, a community group, or a larger organization, I can help you harness the power of your data to create real, lasting change.

Why Work with Me?

A Collaborative Approach

Data consulting isn’t just about numbers—it’s about listening, understanding your needs, and partnering with you every step of the way. I’m committed to making data accessible and meaningful, so you feel empowered to take charge of your story and your impact.

Building Trust Through Transparency

I prioritize clear communication, honesty, and respect for your unique experiences. I’ll walk you through each stage of the process, explaining the technical side in straightforward language to ensure you feel confident and informed.

Transforming Data into Stories

Data should reflect who you are and what you stand for. I specialize in data storytelling that turns complex information into relatable narratives, helping your team connect with the bigger picture and make decisions that truly align with your mission.

Solutions That Embrace Diversity

I bring experience working with diverse clients across various industries, and I’m deeply committed to creating inclusive, adaptable data solutions. My goal is to make sure the tools we create together fit your organization’s unique voice, values, and challenges.
